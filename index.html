Questions From Families Like Yours

We're here to help you make informed decisions about your family's pool journey.

How long does installation take?

Most installations take 2-4 weeks from start to finish, depending on your project's complexity and local conditions. Our dealers work closely with you to create a timeline that minimizes disruption to your family's routine while ensuring quality installation.

What is included in the warranty?

Our lifetime structural warranty covers the pool shell against structural defects for as long as you own your home. We also provide additional warranties for equipment and accessories, giving your family complete peace of mind. Your local dealer will provide detailed warranty information specific to your purchase.

Do you offer financing?

Yes, we partner with several financing companies to offer competitive rates and flexible terms for your pool project. Many families find that monthly payments are more manageable than expected, making their dream pool an affordable reality. Your local dealer can guide you through the financing options available in your area.

How much maintenance is required?

Fiberglass pools require significantly less maintenance than concrete or vinyl pools. Regular cleaning and chemical balancing are still necessary, but the smooth, non-porous surface of fiberglass resists algae growth and requires fewer chemicals. Many families spend just 20-30 minutes per week on basic maintenance, leaving more time for enjoying your pool.

Can I customize my pool?

Absolutely! We offer various customization options including colors, steps, benches, and lighting packages. While the shell shapes are pre-designed for optimal strength and durability, the surrounding deck, landscaping, and features can be completely customized to match your family's vision and complement your home's architecture.

Are your pools safe for children and pets?

Yes, our pools feature textured steps and non-slip surfaces for added safety. Many models include shallow areas perfect for young children and pets. While we design with safety in mind, we always recommend proper supervision and additional safety measures like pool fences, covers, and alarms to create layers of protection for your loved ones.
