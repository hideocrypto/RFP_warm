Our Journey

The story of Rainforest Pools USA is one of family, passion, and a relentless pursuit of creating the perfect backyard paradise.

From Dream to Reality

Rainforest Pools USA began in the early 2000s when founder Michael Ramirez, after years in the pool construction industry, recognized that fiberglass pools offered families something special: a beautiful, durable, low-maintenance way to bring their backyard dreams to life.

What started as a small family operation has grown into one of America's premier fiberglass pool manufacturers, but we've never lost sight of what matters most—creating spaces where families connect and memories are made.

Through economic ups and downs, changing trends, and evolving technology, we've remained committed to our core values: exceptional quality, thoughtful design, and putting families first in everything we do.

Today, thousands of families across the country enjoy their Rainforest pools every day, creating memories that will last generations. We're proud of our history, excited about our future, and grateful for every family that has invited us to be part of their story.
