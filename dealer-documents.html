Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a Rainforest Pools dealer?

We're always looking for passionate professionals to join our dealer network. The process begins with a conversation about your business, market, and vision. We then provide training, resources, and ongoing support to help you succeed. Contact our dealer development team at dealers@rainforestpoolsusa.com to start the conversation.

What training do you provide for new dealers?

New dealers receive comprehensive training covering product knowledge, installation techniques, sales strategies, and customer service best practices. We offer both in-person and virtual training options, as well as ongoing education opportunities to keep your team at the forefront of the industry.

How quickly can pools be delivered to dealers?

Our current lead time for standard models is 4-6 weeks from order confirmation. We maintain a production schedule that prioritizes dealer needs and customer timelines. For special orders or custom features, additional time may be required. Your dedicated account manager can provide specific timeline estimates for your orders.

What marketing support do you offer dealers?

We provide a comprehensive marketing toolkit including high-quality images, customizable brochures, social media content, website materials, and co-op advertising opportunities. Our marketing team is also available to help develop custom campaigns for your specific market and customer base.
